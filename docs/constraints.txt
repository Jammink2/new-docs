# Platform Constraints

Treasure Data is a cloud hosting environment, and as such is subject to some constraints that you may not be used to in a local Hadoop setup. These constraints exist to ensure that your app can take advantage of Treasure Dataâ€™s scalability and redundancy features.

## Hive UDF (User Defined Function)

You cannot add your custom Hive UDF. If you have a request for this specific function, please let us know.

## Hive Custom Map/Reduce Scripts

You cannot use [custom map/reduce scripts](https://cwiki.apache.org/confluence/display/Hive/Tutorial#Tutorial-Custommap%2Freducescripts), due to a security consideration.
