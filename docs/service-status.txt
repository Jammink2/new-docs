# Service Status

## Status Page

[status.treasuredata.com](http://status.treasuredata.com/) provides historical information of our service status. You can also subscribe to our outage / performance degradation event notifications via RSS, email, or SMS. It's recommended for all paid users to subscribe to these events.

## Uptime Page

[uptime.treasuredata.com](http://uptime.treasuredata.com/) provides historical uptime and response time information.
